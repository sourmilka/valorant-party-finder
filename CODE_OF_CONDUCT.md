## 🎯 **Our Pledge**

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in the **VALORANT Party Finder** project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of:

- 🎮 Gaming skill level or rank
- 🌍 Age, body size, disability, ethnicity, or nationality
- 👥 Gender identity and expression
- 🎓 Level of experience or education
- 💼 Socioeconomic status
- 🎨 Personal appearance, race, or religion
- ❤️ Sexual identity and orientation

## ⚡ **Our Standards**

### **✅ Positive Behavior**

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

#### **🎮 In Gaming Context**
- **Encouraging teammates** regardless of their rank or performance
- **Sharing knowledge** about VALORANT strategies and mechanics
- **Being patient** with new players learning the game
- **Celebrating victories** and learning from defeats together
- **Respecting different playstyles** and agent preferences

#### **💻 In Development Context**
- **Using welcoming and inclusive language** in all communications
- **Being respectful** of differing viewpoints and experiences
- **Gracefully accepting constructive criticism** and feedback
- **Focusing on what is best** for the community and project
- **Showing empathy** towards other community members
- **Helping newcomers** understand the codebase and contribution process

### **❌ Unacceptable Behavior**

Examples of unacceptable behavior by participants include:

#### **🚫 Gaming-Related Harassment**
- **Rank shaming** or discriminating based on competitive rank
- **Toxic behavior** similar to what we fight against in-game
- **Agent/role discrimination** or forcing specific picks
- **Griefing** or intentionally disrupting community activities

#### **🚫 General Harassment**
- **Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments** and personal attacks
- **Public or private harassment** of any kind
- **Publishing others' private information** without explicit permission
- **Sexual language or imagery** and unwelcome sexual attention
- **Other conduct** which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## 🛡️ **Enforcement Responsibilities**

### **👮 Community Leaders**

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying the standards of acceptable behavior and are expected to take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any instances of unacceptable behavior.

### **⚖️ Enforcement Powers**

Community leaders have the right and responsibility to:
- **Remove, edit, or reject** comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct
- **Temporarily or permanently ban** any contributor for other behaviors that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful

## 🌍 **Scope**

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces, and also applies when an individual is officially representing the community in public spaces. Examples include:

### **📍 Where This Applies**
- **GitHub repository** (issues, pull requests, discussions)
- **Discord server** and other chat platforms
- **Social media** when representing the project
- **Gaming sessions** organized through the platform
- **Community events** and meetups
- **Email communications** with maintainers

## 🚨 **Reporting**

### **📞 How to Report**

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the community leaders responsible for enforcement at:

- **Email**: [conduct@valorant-party-finder.com](mailto:conduct@valorant-party-finder.com)
- **Discord**: Direct message to moderators
- **GitHub**: Private message to repository maintainers

### **🔒 Confidentiality**

All complaints will be:
- **Reviewed and investigated** promptly and fairly
- **Kept confidential** to the extent possible
- **Handled with discretion** and respect for all parties involved

All community leaders are obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter of any incident.

## ⚖️ **Enforcement Guidelines**

Community leaders will follow these Community Impact Guidelines in determining the consequences for any action they deem in violation of this Code of Conduct:

### **1. 🟡 Correction**

**Community Impact**: Use of inappropriate language or other behavior deemed unprofessional or unwelcome in the community.

**Consequence**: A private, written warning from community leaders, providing clarity around the nature of the violation and an explanation of why the behavior was inappropriate. A public apology may be requested.

### **2. 🟠 Warning**

**Community Impact**: A violation through a single incident or series of actions.

**Consequence**: A warning with consequences for continued behavior. No interaction with the people involved, including unsolicited interaction with those enforcing the Code of Conduct, for a specified period of time. This includes avoiding interactions in community spaces as well as external channels like social media. Violating these terms may lead to a temporary or permanent ban.

### **3. 🔴 Temporary Ban**

**Community Impact**: A serious violation of community standards, including sustained inappropriate behavior.

**Consequence**: A temporary ban from any sort of interaction or public communication with the community for a specified period of time. No public or private interaction with the people involved, including unsolicited interaction with those enforcing the Code of Conduct, is allowed during this period. Violating these terms may lead to a permanent ban.

### **4. ⚫ Permanent Ban**

**Community Impact**: Demonstrating a pattern of violation of community standards, including sustained inappropriate behavior, harassment of an individual, or aggression toward or disparagement of classes of individuals.

**Consequence**: A permanent ban from any sort of public interaction within the community.

## 🎮 **Gaming-Specific Guidelines**

### **🏆 Competitive Integrity**

- **Respect all skill levels** - from Iron to Radiant
- **No boosting or account sharing** discussions
- **Fair play** - no cheating or exploit discussions
- **Positive team environment** - encourage and support teammates

### **🎯 Agent and Role Respect**

- **All agents are valid** - no agent discrimination
- **Respect role preferences** - don't force specific picks
- **Constructive feedback only** - help players improve
- **Team composition discussions** should be respectful

### **🌐 Regional and Cultural Sensitivity**

- **Respect different regions** and their playstyles
- **No region baiting** or discrimination
- **Language barriers** - be patient and helpful
- **Cultural differences** - embrace diversity in the community

## 🤝 **Positive Community Building**

### **🌟 Encouraging Participation**

- **Welcome new members** warmly
- **Share knowledge freely** about VALORANT and development
- **Celebrate contributions** of all sizes
- **Create inclusive discussions** that everyone can join

### **🎯 Focus on Growth**

- **Learning environment** - mistakes are opportunities to grow
- **Constructive criticism** - focus on improvement, not blame
- **Skill development** - help others improve their game and code
- **Community goals** - work together toward common objectives

## 📚 **Resources**

### **🆘 Getting Help**

If you're experiencing harassment or need support:
- **Immediate safety**: Contact local authorities if needed
- **Community support**: Reach out to moderators or maintainers
- **Mental health**: Consider professional support if needed
